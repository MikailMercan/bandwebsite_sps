About

Seven Page Skripture is a metalcore band stationed in Leuven, Belgium. The project itself started in the beginning of 2021. With a full line-up now being present we officially accept gigs in the whole region of Belgium as well as beyond. If these couple sentences have already convinced you, then feel free to book us now!
